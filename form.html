Guidelines for the Presentation

  1. Please use ppt format for making the presentation
  2. The pressentation should not be more than 10 slides
  3. Please structure your presentation to include these:
    • Concept / Idea / Problem Solved
    • Vision / Solution
    • Technology Stack and sourcing (in house or outsourced)
    • Any intellectual property (IP) registered
    • Management Team
    • Product Readiness / Roadmap
    • Current Funding
    • Financial Projections for next two years
    • Competitive Landscape
    • Customers Profile
    • Scale Up Plan
  4. Please feel free to include any demo URLs and videos for your products
