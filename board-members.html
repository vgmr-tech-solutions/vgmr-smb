Chetan Kapoor

Travel Industry Analyst

(India)

Chetan Kapoor has a deep understanding of the the travel and tourism business. A journalist-turned-analyst, Chetan is a highly sought after speaker and an authority on global travel trends. Over the last decade, Chetan has interviewed several founders and CEOs of leading travel companies, authored industry reports on the digital travel trends, global online travel agency landscape, travel behavior, and analyzed funding & product evolution among travel startups.

Chetan is also a Senior Research Analyst at Phocuswright, a renowned travel industry research firm. In 2017, Chetan co-founded VIDEC to engage with the travel and tourism industry across market entry and expansion strategies, data analysis, corporate development and more..
