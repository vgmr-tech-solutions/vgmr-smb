“Start-up Mentor Board”

"Start-up Mentor Board" mentors, guides and helps Start-ups in the Travel, Tourism and Hospitality Industry by well known business leaders for Technology, Digital, Business Transformation, Collaborations & Alliances, at both National and Global level.

What’s in for you as a Start-up

MENTORING WHERE YOUR IDEAS WILL BE GUIDED BY EXPERTS OF DIFFERENT DOMAINS
BUSINESS ADVISORY TO SCALE UP TAILORED CONSULTING FOR STARTUP OPERATION
COLLABORATIONS AND ALLIANCES WITH THE GLOBAL TRAVEL ECOSYSTEM
BUILD NETWORKS FROM STRONG MANAGEMENT SYSTEM
TECHNOLOGY COLLABORATION, SUGGESTIONS AND RECOMMENDATIONS

“Starts ups need to mesh in with the existing travel eco-system and existing travel enterprise need to realise the importance of what these start-ups bring to the universe of travel . This should have happened a long time ago” - Ashish Kumar

About Startup Mentor Board (SMB)

A visionary global board with strong credentials in Business, Technology, ECommerce, Digital, Emerging Technologies, Market Place, Payment Solutions and IT Infrastructure and an Integration with the larger Global Travel Eco-System

The mentor board is spearheaded by Ashish Kumar, who is also the Co-Chairman of FICCI Travel technology committee, and comprises of experts from both Indian and global travel industry, bringing together a wealth of knowledge experience and expertise. The board has affiliations in Europe, Africa and would gradually expand it’s reach to other global travel markets, where start-up require nurturing and fostering.
